Provision the jail from an ix-iocage-plugin.

Installable ix-iocage plugins can be found on the registry repository https://github.com/freenas/iocage-ix-plugins/ and may be configured as a jails provisioning.source property, e.g.:

ioc set provisioning.method=ix provisioning.source=jenkins myjail
